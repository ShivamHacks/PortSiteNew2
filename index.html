Shivam Agrawal

Highschooler • Developer • Explorer

Hello! I'm Shivam Agrawal, a highschool programmer who loves computer science, not only for its capability to automate my homework, but also its capacity to connect people, provide navigation through traffic, and locate food. After hacking for a few months, I began to truly program, moving beyond the basic commands and understanding computer science fundamentals such as algorithms and data structures. Once I had learned some of the major concepts, I began to apply them to several projects I had been planning to create. Soon after my first deploy, I realized the thrill of programming. I built more and more, and kept gaining experience. Now I see the world and many of its problems as computer solutions yet to be created. Combining this with business insight gained from various school clubs, an internship, and a job, I was able to acquire a different view of technology, one that allows me not only to create, but also to consider the why, in essence, the business questions. For me, programming has now evolved into Think • Plan • Build • Deploy.
